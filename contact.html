Chiramel Luxury Auditorium

Thank you for considering Chiramel Auditorium as the venue for your special event. Located in the heart of Vaikom, our auditorium stands as a beacon of elegance and convenience for various occasions, including marriages, parties, receptions, cultural events, performing arts, and social gatherings. Established in 2017, Chiramel Auditorium is renowned as one of the premier event spaces in the area, boasting state-of-the-art facilities and the charm of a centrally air-conditioned ambiance. Situated conveniently opposite Udayanapuram Homeopathy Hospital and merely 1 kilometer away from the revered Udayanapuram Temple, our location offers unparalleled accessibility to both locals and visitors alike. Whether you're planning a grand celebration or an intimate gathering, our dedicated team is here to ensure your event is nothing short of spectacular. For inquiries, bookings, or to schedule a tour of our facilities, please feel free to reach out to us using the contact details provided below. We look forward to assisting you in creating unforgettable memories at Chiramel Auditorium.
