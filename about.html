About Us

Chiramel Luxury Auditorium
Enjoy a Luxury Experience

"Welcome to Chiramel Auditorium, your premier destination for memorable events in Vaikom! Nestled conveniently opposite Udayanapuram Homeopathy Hospital, and just 1km away from Udayanapuram Temple, our venue offers the perfect setting for weddings, parties, receptions, cultural events, and more.


Since our establishment in 2017, Chiramel Auditorium has been renowned as one of the finest centrally air-conditioned venues in Vaikom, boasting spacious facilities and easy accessibility to all parts of the city and beyond. We look forward to hosting your next unforgettable gathering!"
